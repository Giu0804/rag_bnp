# DIAPO 1 DATA

**Title: Data Panorama: Diversity and Complexity for RAG**

**Objective of the selected data:**
Go beyond simple single-paragraph extraction (Single-hop).
Provide corpora requiring the cross-referencing of multiple textual sources.

**Structural challenges of the corpora:**
Multi-hop reasoning: Linking facts that require connecting multiple documents together.
Volume and density: Handling very long contexts (up to 11,000 tokens) or large document collections (Top-100 passages).

**Our 4 working datasets:**
HotpotQA: Wikipedia-based corpus (113k Q/A) focused on navigating between related concepts.

MuSiQue: Question graphs (2 to 4 hops) built by assembling existing datasets.

QAMPARI (ALCE): Complex queries derived from knowledge graphs (Wikidata/Tables), producing answers in the form of entity lists.

RAGBench: Aggregation of 100k examples drawn from 5 industry sectors (legal, medical, finance, technical support, general knowledge).
